About us

About the company


We are a global online takeaway food ordering marketplace, with a focus on emerging markets. The KrazzyKhana group is the leading online food delivery marketplace in emerging markets. It enables restaurants to become visible in the online and mobile world and provides them with an industry leading software and technology to generate additional demand. For consumers, KrazzyKhana offers the convenience to order food online and the widest gastronomic range, from which they can choose their favourite meal via app or online with a few fingertips.

Key facts


  • Founded in February 2019
  • Operating across three continents
  • Active in more than 450 cities around the world
  • Partners with more than 26,000 restaurants globally
  • With a team of more than 2,500 people worldwide
  • Headquartered in Nagpur
